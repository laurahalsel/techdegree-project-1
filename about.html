Story Time

It all started...

Well, let' see. I was adopted at 4 years old, shipped to the Americas from Thailand. Unfortunately, I will never know my birth parents because I was dumped in front of a hospital. Don't fee sad though. I was adopted by a great family who have allowed me to try anything I wanted. I was always the artsy fartsy type. I would make puppets out of cardboards, run around pretending to be Simba, and drawing whatever I imagined.

Let's fast forward to when I went to college. As stated before, I changed my majors three times. I majored in:

  • Criminal Justice
  • Social Work
  • and Communication Design a.k.a. graphic design

How I ended up taking this techdegree course? I can tell you it took a lot of exploring. I started out at your typical retail jobs, thought I wanted to be a chef but ditched that idea in a couple months. I have worked at many places, excelling at those places, but just not finding my nitch. You are always told:

  1. Graduate high school
  2. Go to college
  3. Find your dream job
  4. Get married and have 2.5 kids

And in that order. I'm still working on number three. Number four will happen just not the kid part.

Although I may have had several jobs, it only allowed me to have gain invaluable experience. I am currently a Production Designer at a small ad agency and I plan to move to Oregon one day.
